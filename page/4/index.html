Python __slot__变量

  • ​ Python可以在定义类中添加特殊变量 __slot__ 来限制该类可以添加的属性，例：

    • 类中绑定两个变量name和age，其他额外定义的变量则不起作用。

    • 由于score没有被放到__slots__中，所以不能绑定score属性，试图绑定score将得到AttributeError的错误。

    • __slot__定义的属性仅对当前的类起作用，对继承的子类不起作用。

Nuclear mass excess and decay Q value

Mass excess and decay Q value

  • The Q value of decay process is the difference between the masse of the
    mother nucleus and the sum of the masses of the final products, in energy units (usually
    in MeV). This is also the corresponding difference of the binding energies of the nuclei (not
    per nucleon), since nucleon number is conserved in a reaction.

  • The masses may be provided in a table of mass excesses $\Delta M(A,Z)​$ , which is the value of $M(A, Z) − Am_u​$ (usually in MeV), but relative to the corresponding number for the isotope $^{12}C​$.

  • $\Delta M(A,Z)=931.478\times(M(A, Z) − Am_u)(1m_u=931.478 MeV)$

  • For $\alpha$ decay Q value can be writen as:

    $Q_\alpha = \Delta_p-\Delta_d-\Delta_{He}$

    $\Delta_{He}=2.425 MeV$

  • Consider the electronic binding energy, we have to plus the correction:

    $\Delta M(A,Z)=931.478\times(M(A, Z) − Am_u)+a_{el}(Z^{2.39}-(Z-2)^{2.39})$

    $a_{el}=1.433\times 10^{-5}MeV$

    For details refer to : Atomic Data and Nuclear Data Tables 77, 311–381 (2001)
